Library Cards

Benefits of Owning a Library Card
  • Check out print and audiovisual materials
  • Use online databases subcribed to by SCPL and the SC State Library
  • Get access to public computers for internet searching and Microsoft Office tasks
  • Download materials and streaming services (some restrictions apply)
  • Request materials from your home computer or mobile device
  • Book one of the available meeting rooms (some restrictions apply)
  • Get avvess to Interlibrary Loan Services (some restrictions apply)
